Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder
  • All purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Egg
  • Heavy cream or fresh cream
  • Ghee or clarified butter
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom pods
  • Oil for frying

Recipe

  1. MAKE THE DOUGH

    Mix wet and dry ingredients separately, then combine to form a dough. Knead, refrigerate for 20 minutes, then knead again until smooth.

  2. FORM BALLS

    Divide the dough into small balls, ensuring they are smooth with no cracks.

  3. FRY THE GULAB JAMUN

    Heat oil to medium and fry the dough balls until golden brown.

  4. MAKE THE SUGAR SYRUP

    Simmer sugar, water, and cardamom until the sugar dissolves.

  5. SOAK THE GULAB JAMUN

    Soak fried balls in syrup for at least 30 minutes.

  6. SERVE & ENJOY!

    Best served warm.

Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Egg yolks
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (optional)

Recipe

  1. COOK THE SPAGHETTI

    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente. Reserve some of the pasta water, then drain the pasta.

  2. PREPARE THE PANCETTA

    In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook until crispy. If using garlic, add it now and cook until fragrant.

  3. MIX THE SAUCE

    In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and grated Parmesan cheese. Add a pinch of salt and a generous amount of black pepper.

  4. COMBINE PASTA AND SAUCE

    Add the hot, drained pasta to the skillet with the pancetta. Remove from heat and pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing quickly to create a creamy sauce. If needed, add reserved pasta water to achieve desired consistency.

  5. SERVE IMMEDIATELY

    Plate the pasta and sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese and black pepper. Serve immediately.
